NoTimeMover already has a **functional marketing landing page** and a **five-step booking flow**. This engagement **extends** that foundation: additional on-page structure and copy to support **trust and clarity** (service areas, FAQ, pricing narrative), **on-page SEO** fundamentals, **analytics and UTM conventions** for attribution, and **Google Business Profile (GBP)** setup and optimization support. You will complete **Google-led verification** steps (e.g. postcard or video) when required.

This document defines **scope, responsibilities, deliverables, and payment terms**. The **bundled project fee** in Section 9 is **separate** from any **monthly retainer** (see **§9.4**)—retainer covers ongoing care, not this build-out.

For the **bundled engagement**, scheduled work commences following **signed acceptance** and receipt of **payment in full (PIF)** as shown in Section 9.

## 3. Scope of services

### 3.1 Website build-out (on-page)

Structured work on the **existing** NoTimeMover property (no separate greenfield site unless later scoped). Deliverables include:

- **Layout & flow**
  - Extend the **single-page** experience from hero through footer with **new sections** aligned to your intake (e.g. trust, process, differentiation vs. “traditional” movers).
  - **Preserve** the booking modal as the primary conversion path unless we agree a small **above-the-fold CTA** tweak.
  - **Mobile-first** spacing and readability pass on **new and touched** sections.

- **Copy & messaging**
  - Refresh **headlines**, **subheads**, and body copy for clarity and **Massachusetts / Boston-area** relevance.
  - **Service-area** block(s): cities, regions, or ZIP-level messaging **you** approve (accuracy is on you).
  - **FAQ** module (question set driven by intake—pricing, coverage, what’s included, deposits/cancellations where applicable).
  - Short **“how pricing works” / transparency** copy consistent with your booking flow (no exposure of internal tier math per current product rules).
  - **Call-to-action** refinement (e.g. booking vs. quote language) coordinated with you.

- **On-page SEO**
  - **Unique page title and meta description** tuned for primary local intent (within reasonable competition—no ranking guarantees).
  - **Heading hierarchy** (`h1`–`h3`) and anchor-friendly section IDs for deep links.
  - **Internal on-page links** where helpful (jump links, repeated CTA paths).
  - **`alt` text guidelines** for images **you** supply (logo, truck, team—stock only if you approve).
  - **FAQ structured data** (JSON-LD or platform-appropriate markup) where technically supported.

- **Measurement & attribution**
  - **Google Analytics 4** property connection (or use of existing property with your access).
  - **Key events**: e.g. modal open, step progression, successful submit (exact list finalized at build).
  - Written **UTM naming convention** plus **example URLs** for Instagram, Marketplace, cross-links from NoTimeStorage, realtor partners, etc.

- **Quality assurance & handoff**
  - Targeted **QA** on shipped changes (modern **mobile + desktop** browsers).
  - Lightweight **handoff**: how to request **small post-launch edits** (especially if you maintain a **monthly retainer**—see §9).

### 3.2 Google Business Profile (GBP)

Hands-on support so your **local presence** matches the website and your **real** operations. Deliverables include:

- **Profile foundation**
  - Guidance on **primary category** and **additional categories** permitted by Google for your service mix.
  - **Business description** draft (character limits, differentiation, no unverifiable superlatives unless you approve).
  - **Hours**, **phone**, **website URL**, and **booking / leads** link alignment with the live site.

- **Service areas & services**
  - **Service area** definition consistent with intake (geographic truth—**you** confirm).
  - List of **services/products** to surface on the profile (e.g. local moving, packing—**only** what you actually offer).

- **Optimization & trust**
  - Recommended **attributes** (where available) to improve relevance and trust.
  - **Photo checklist**: logo, cover, truck/crew, before/after (where appropriate)—**you** upload; I specify sizes and priority.
  - **Q&A seed list** (neutral, factual answers you approve) to reduce repetitive inbound questions.

- **Reviews & reputation**
  - Copy template for **post-job Google review requests** (timing and channel per your workflow).

- **Verification**
  - Step-by-step **instructions** for **postcard, phone, or video** verification when Google requires it—**you** must complete identity steps; I cannot impersonate your business.

**Third-party caveat:** GBP outcomes depend on **Google’s policies, verification queues, and data quality**. Consultant does not warrant **approval timelines**, verification outcomes, or continued eligibility if listing data is inaccurate or policy changes occur. **Accuracy of published business facts remains Client’s responsibility.**
