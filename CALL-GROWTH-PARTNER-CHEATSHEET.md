## 2) Qualifying questions — ask these before anything else

These are non-negotiable. Don't skip them because he's excited.

| Topic | Ask | What you're listening for |
|---|---|---|
| **Consistency** | How many moves have you done in the last 30 days? | Any answer is fine — you need a number, not "a few" |
| **Current volume** | How many jobs do you have lined up right now or this week? | Tests whether he's actually operational vs. occasional |
| **Capacity ceiling** | If 10 leads came in this week, what's your max — how many could you physically take? | Tells you when the system breaks and whether he needs crew now |
| **Commitment** | Are you treating this as your main thing right now or is it alongside other work? | If it's a side thing, speed-to-lead doesn't matter — he won't respond fast anyway |
| **Crew** | Are you doing every move solo or do you have anyone you can call? | Solo operator = hard ceiling on scale |
| **Equipment** | What truck/van do you have? Is it yours or rented? | Affects which job sizes he can take — important for the quote tool |

**Qualify/disqualify signal:** If he can't give you a number for moves in the last 30 days, has no jobs lined up, and is doing this alongside a full-time job — pump the brakes on the full build. Do a smaller scoped engagement first.

## 4) Current process audit — how leads move through his hands right now

You need this to build the CRM and automation correctly. Don't assume anything.

| Topic | Ask |
|---|---|
| **How he finds out about a lead** | When someone reaches out right now, how does that actually reach you — text, call, email, DM? |
| **Response time** | How fast do you typically get back to people — same day, within the hour, depends? |
| **What he says** | When you respond to a new enquiry, what do you actually say or do first — call them, send a price, ask questions? |
| **What info he still needs** | What do you always have to ask that people don't tell you upfront — date, stairs, distance, how much stuff? |
| **How a job gets confirmed** | Walk me through what happens between first contact and the job being locked in — what's that sequence? |
| **Payment** | When and how do you collect — before, day-of, after? Cash, Zelle, Venmo, something else? Any deposit? |
| **No-shows / cancellations** | Have you had anyone cancel or ghost after confirming? How did you handle it? |
