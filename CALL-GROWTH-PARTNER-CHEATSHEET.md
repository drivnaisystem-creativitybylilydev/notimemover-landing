# Call cheat sheet — Jermaine (growth partner frame)

**Your role today:** Discovery + priority. **Not** to design a marketplace on the phone.  
**His reality:** No moving clients yet; may cross-post from **other business**; long-term **marketplace** vision.  
**Your anchor:** Validate **first real moves** before “platform.”

---

## 0) First 60 seconds (say this)

“We’ve got the landing baseline. Today I want to understand **how you want to win the next 10 conversations**—speed, area, offer, follow-up—then I’ll recommend **one or two** next investments. I’ll follow up with **2–3 fixed-price options** so nothing’s fuzzy.”

---

## 1) Three outcomes for *this* call (check mentally)

- **Near-term goal** is specific (e.g. “10 qualified form fills in 30 days” / “5 booked estimates”).
- **One primary channel** to try first (not five).
- **Next step** = you send **written packages + prices** within 48h.

---

## 2) Questions (pick ~6; don’t interrogate)


| Topic          | Ask                                                                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Geo**        | Where do you *actually* want jobs? Any hard nos?                         |
| **Capacity**   | Leads per week before you’re overwhelmed?                                |
| **Follow-up**  | How fast do you respond—minutes, hours, days?                            |
| **Proof**      | License/insurance, photos, testimonials from *any* work?                 |
| **Offer**      | What’s the wedge vs big brands—in one sentence?                          |
| **Money**      | Comfort level: **your fees** vs **monthly ad spend** (separate buckets)? |
| **Other page** | What’s allowed to cross-post—link, CTA, frequency?                       |


---

## 3) What you recommend (say in this order)

1. **Message + friction** — Page clear on **who / where / what happens next** (already mostly there).
2. **Lead discipline** — Email + Sheet = fine; **he** owns speed-to-lead.
3. **UTMs** — One tracked link when he pushes from **other business** (`utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`).
4. **GBP** — **Google Business Profile** for *this* brand when eligible; photos + categories + areas; reviews after job one.
5. **Traffic-ready** — Tags/events **before** serious ad spend (so clicks aren’t blind).
6. **Paid test** — After (5): small **steady** budget, usually **intent** first; Meta if strong creative later.
7. **SEO expansion** — **FAQ + areas + structured facts** on current page → **multi-page** only if he commits inputs + timeline (months).

**Marketplace:** “Love the vision—it’s **separate milestones** from getting move #1–#10; we can scope a **paid roadmap doc** without building blindly.”

---

## 4) If he goes off-script (one-liners)


| He says…                    | You say…                                                                                     |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| “No clients yet”            | “Then we optimize for **trust + clarity** until review #1 lands.”                            |
| “Push from other page only” | “Good for **awareness**—we measure with **UTMs** and still need **intent** long-term.”       |
| “Marketplace ASAP”          | “That’s **product work**—we start with **roadmap + proof** you can originate demand/supply.” |
| “Just run ads”              | “Ads need **follow-up + tracking**—otherwise we burn budget learning nothing.”               |


---

## 5) Pricing you can quote (ranges → finalize in writing)

**One-time**


| Package                                                             | Rough range    |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| **Traffic-ready** (measurement + UTMs + conversion QA)              | **$750–$2k**   |
| **Local launch** (GBP plan + FAQ/area copy on page + schema basics) | **$1k–$2.5k**  |
| **Content sprint** (2–4 pages; *needs his inputs*)                  | **$1.5k–$4k**  |
| **Marketplace roadmap** (workshop + written phases/risks; no code)  | **$500–$1.5k** |


**Monthly** (separate from his **$49** site-care if you keep that lane narrow)


| Offer                                                                      | Rough range                                  |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Growth retainer** (touchpoint + small implementation budget; define hrs) | **$500–$1.5k/mo**                            |
| **Ads management** (**his spend** on his card + your ops fee)              | **$500–$2k/mo** + minimum ad budget you name |


---

## 6) What you sell *vs* what he sells

**You:** Site, fixes, funnel plumbing, analytics setup, advising, scoped channel help.  
**He:** Offers, proofs, approvals, creative assets, money for platforms, **answering the phone.**

---

## 7) Close the call (verbatim)

“Here’s what I’m taking away: **[repeat his goal]**.”  
“I’m going to send **Starter / Recommended / Stretch** tomorrow with **scope + price**.”  
“What I need from you by **[date]**:** **[FAQ list / areas / photos / logo / access]**.”

---

## 8) If you freeze — default move

**Pause → one question → one recommendation.**  
Example: “What’s the #1 number you want higher in 30 days—**leads**, **shows**, or **booked moves**?” → recommend **traffic-ready OR local launch OR tiny ad test** from section 3.
